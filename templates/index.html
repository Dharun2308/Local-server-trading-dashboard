Sell Covered Call

Sell a call against 100+ shares you own to collect premium. Pick an OTM strike (above spot) to keep upside room; ITM caps your gains. Best when IV rank is high — the chip warns (never blocks) if premium is cheap. The chaser walks your credit down from limit to floor until it fills.
Pick a strike to see its premium.

Covered Call Roller

Move an existing short call to a new strike/expiration for a net credit — use it to dodge assignment or buy more time. The chaser walks the credit down to your floor. If a roll won't pay a credit, you may be rolling too far; widen the expiration or move the strike closer.
Pick a target strike to see its mid.

Premium Yield

For each short option: the annualized return on the strike from the remaining time value (intrinsic value isn't income — it's assignment exposure). High % = still paying you, let it ride. Low % = premium nearly gone, roll or close to redeploy the capital. ITM = assignment risk. Annualized so a 7-day and a 45-day position compare fairly.
